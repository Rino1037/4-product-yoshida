LaRosa's About Cafe and Bar Menus

LaRosa's is a blend between Italian and American cuisines, with various blends of plates from our cafe menu to our bar menu. Established in the 90's LaRosa's has always been a family favorite and continues to be even till this day. With each bite of intense flavor it'll keep you asking for more, everyone loves LaRosas from babies to the elderly. We aspire to make our customers satisfied no matter what. Paul LaRosa, the owner, is a friendly man and makes sure his guests are happy and most importantly full.
